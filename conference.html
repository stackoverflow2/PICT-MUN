League Of Nations

Agenda :

The Spread Of Nazism in European regions

  • The League of Nations was what you could call a pre-UN. It was formed with the same motive as the United Nations but it failed majorly due to certain setbacks (such as, ever member state had a veto, thus no resolution could effectively be passed).
  • This historical committee shall focus on how to work with these conditions on the agenda where Nazism is strongly spreading through Europe and how to stop this, during the Second World War.

Chairperson:

Saras Bansal

United Nations General Assembly (ECOFIN)

Agenda :

Reviewing Global Bank Policies to avert Recessions

  • Since the major economical setbacks in North America as well as Europe, the UNGA ECOFIN will meet and discuss what measure member states can come up with to avoid such a drastic recession again.
  • The committee shall focus on discussing and amending the Global Bank Policies so as to best avert another recession.

Chairperson:

Sakshi Sohoni

United Nations Security Council

Agenda :

The South China Sea conflict

  • The conflict or dispute about the South China Sea has been going on for a very long time. Basically, it is a territorial dispute between a set of countries, where they're all fighting for claim of several regions of the South China Sea.
  • Many countries want it to be an international border so that the high trade through the sea can function as it is, whereas others want it to get inside their borders.
  • The committee shall focus on how best to try and resolve this issue and will look at it from several angles and make sure a sufficient conclusion can be come up with.

Chairperson:

To be declared

United Nations Human Rights Council

Agenda :

The Violation Of Human Rights inside the Syrian region

  • The war between the Syrian government and the Rebels has been going on for a really long time now. Due to this civil war, the civilians still living in that area are suffering through horrible conditions, such conditions that no human should suffer through. They are being denied basic human rights.
  • The committee will focus on coming to a conclusion as to how to stop this situation from worsening and how to provide comfort to those suffering there.

Chairperson:

Raavi Uttarwar

Welcome to the first ever edition of PICT MUN. Here, we hope to build a budding and growing relationship with important aspects of a successful life, passion, hard work and confidence. We believe and emphasize on the necessity to create awareness on international affairs, a need to understand deeply the consequences and impacts each event has on our lives. PICT MUN hopes to bring about a real impact in the lives of India’s youth, an impact that motivates us to bring our country joy and glory. We want to establish in our youth, the deep understanding of our world as it is today. By introducing the youth to leadership skills, diplomacy and a critically analytical mindset, we hope to produce leaders that could change the face of earth, leaders who will help attain international peace and global understanding. And thus, it is our honour, to present you with the first very conference of PICT MUN that could lead to an exciting journey, a journey lasting much longer that just two days. Join us, to change lives.
